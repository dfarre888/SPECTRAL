Ukrainian-Russian War Drone Aircraft\n\n## Aviation \\| Military History\n\n### Drones have seen increasiny use in the skies over Ukraine following the Russian invasion of February 2022.\n\nThe Ukrainian-Russian War of 2022 represents the first large-scale use of drone aircraft in warfare between two Top 20 global military powers. Chief contributors include the Turkish-made Bayraktar TB2 attack drone and the Russian Orlan-10 as well as some off-the-shelf contributions originating from China.\n\nThere are a total of \\[ 41 \\] Ukrainian-Russian War Drone Aircraft entries in the Military Factory.
