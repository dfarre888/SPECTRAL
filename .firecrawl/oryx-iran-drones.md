Consisting of radio-controlled Mohajer-1s [armed](https://postlmg.cc/dkQtBmM3) with up to six RPG-7, Iran's drone arsenal has since expanded to include copies of the U.S. RQ-170 Sentinel, PGM-toting (stealth) UCAVs and highly accurate loitering muntions. These types set the stage for an increasingly effective replication of legacy aerial assets, a fact that has not gone unnoticed by an increasing number of customers for Iranian drone designs.\n\nThe goal of this list is to comprehensively catalogue Iran's current inventory of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and their armament.\nIn an effort to streamline the list and avoid unnecessary confusion,\nthis list only includes military drones associated with Iran's defence\nindustry or military-grade UAVs with at least some chance of entering\nservice. The part within the apostrophes refers to other designations or an\nunofficial designation. Dubious claims are referred to as ''claimed to\nbe capable''.\n\n(Click on the drones and their armament to get a picture of them)\n\n### Surveillance Unmanned Aerial Vehicles - Operational\n\n-
